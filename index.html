What Makes Us Different

We're building a self-sustaining educational community where knowledge is freely shared and everyone both receives and contributes value.

Peer-Driven Learning

Participants both learn from and teach each other, creating a dynamic and engaging educational experience.

Built-in Mentorship

Our overlapping cohort design naturally creates mentorship opportunities at multiple stages of learning.

Zero Financial Barriers

No tuition or fees—participants "pay" through their contributions to the collective and future cohorts.

How It Works

Our unique approach creates a sustainable learning ecosystem where everyone both receives and contributes value.

Apply and Join a Cohort

Submit your application for our selective process. Accepted members join a cohort of peers with complementary skills and interests.

12-Week Intensive Learning

Participate in a structured curriculum with projects, presentations, and peer learning. In the second half, mentor newer members while advancing your skills.

Complete Projects and Portfolio

Build industry-relevant projects that demonstrate your expertise and can be added to your professional portfolio.

Give Back to the Collective

After graduation, contribute to the next cohort through mentorship, curriculum improvements, and career support—maintaining the sustainable learning ecosystem.
